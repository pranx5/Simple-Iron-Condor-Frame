Starting point (suggestion)

Suggested short band: ±$— → —

Intraday 1SD uses DTE = 1 trading day (0DTE end-of-day context); formula: price × (IV/100) / √252. The slider scales how far suggested shorts sit from spot as a fraction of that 1SD move.

~68% model probability inside suggested shorts at 1.0×1SD (normal approx.). Adjust slider to change band width.

Lower = tighter shorts (more aggressive / less room to spot); 1.0 = full ±1SD band used for suggestions.

Pre-filled default only; 0DTE IV is often well above headline VIX—override with IV from your chain. For SPX, VIX is often a reasonable starting proxy for ATM IV (not exact).

Trade log (saved on disk)

Log iron condors from Thinkorswim. npm start saves to data/trades.json on disk. With python -m http.server only, trades are stored in this browser (localStorage). Four legs, one per line: strike then CALL or PUT.
